Mira is designed for enterprise use with strong security and compliance foundations.

## Data protection

### GDPR compliance

Mira is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This includes:

- Data processing agreements available upon request
- Right to access, correct, and delete personal data
- Data minimization principles applied throughout the platform

### Zero Data Retention (ZDR)

For organizations with strict data handling requirements, Mira offers optional **Zero Data Retention** mode. When enabled, candidate data is not persisted after your session ends.

<Info>
Contact [support@mira.day](mailto:support@mira.day) to enable ZDR for your account.
</Info>

## Infrastructure security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Authentication via Google, Microsoft SSO, or email verification
- Role-based access controls for team workspaces

## Data isolation

- Your training data and preferences are scoped to your account
- Candidate search results are not shared across users
- Task data is accessible only to you and team members you explicitly share with

## Responsible AI

Mira uses state-of-the-art AI models with safeguards designed to:
- Avoid bias in candidate evaluation
- Provide transparent reasoning for recommendations
- Let you maintain final decision authority over all hiring actions
