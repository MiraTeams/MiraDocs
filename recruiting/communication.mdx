## Outreach capabilities

Mira supports direct candidate outreach through multiple channels:

### Email

Compose and send personalized emails to candidates directly from within a task. Mira can:
- Draft outreach messages based on the candidate's background and the role
- Personalize messaging at scale
- Track email delivery and responses

### Calls

Mira supports outbound call workflows to connect with candidates directly.

## Automatic record linking

All communication — emails sent, calls made, responses received — is automatically linked to the corresponding candidate profile within the task. This means:

- Complete interaction history per candidate
- No manual data entry or CRM updates
- Full audit trail for compliance

## Outreach best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personalize every message">
    Mira can draft personalized outreach based on each candidate's specific background. Mention their current role, recent achievements, or relevant skills.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reach out promptly">
    Top candidates are often off the market quickly. Use Mira's prioritization to identify and contact high-match candidates first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Follow up consistently">
    Set up follow-up reminders within tasks to ensure no candidate falls through the cracks.
  </Accordion>
