## Overview

Mira is an AI agent that works alongside you. You describe what you need in natural language, and Mira autonomously executes multi-step workflows to deliver results.

## Workflow

When you submit a message in a task, Mira follows this process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understand your request">
    Mira analyzes your description — extracting role requirements, skills, location preferences, seniority level, and other criteria. If anything is unclear, Mira may ask clarifying questions before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and aggregate">
    Mira runs targeted searches across multiple data sources:
    - **People Search** — Finds candidate profiles matching your criteria
    - **People Data Generation** — Compiles structured candidate profiles from raw search results
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present results">
    Results are delivered in multiple formats:
    - **Summary text** — High-level analysis and recommendations
    - **Data tables** — Structured comparisons you can copy, download, or view fullscreen
    - **Downloadable files** — Complete candidate datasets in JSON format (typically 30 candidates per search)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterate with you">
    You can refine results through follow-up messages. Mira retains full context within the task, so each refinement builds on previous results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Mira can do

Mira supports a wide range of recruiting-related tasks:

- **Find candidates** — Describe a role and get a list of matching candidates
- **Filter and refine** — Narrow results by adding criteria ("only those with published papers")
- **Evaluate and rank** — Ask Mira to prioritize candidates and explain why
- **Generate outreach** — Draft personalized messages for specific candidates
- **Research roles** — Get market analysis and competitive context for a position
- **Analyze profiles** — Deep-dive into specific candidates' backgrounds

## Supported languages

Mira understands and responds in both **English** and **Chinese**. You can write in either language and Mira will respond accordingly.
