## Built-in recruiting skills

Mira comes with specialized skills designed for the recruiting workflow:

### Role clarification

When you describe a position, Mira can ask clarifying questions to ensure it understands the requirements — seniority level, must-have vs. nice-to-have skills, location preferences, and compensation range.

### Candidate evaluation

Mira evaluates candidates against your requirements and provides:
- **Match analysis** — How well each candidate fits the role
- **Tiered recommendations** — Candidates grouped by match quality (e.g., "Tier 1 — Extremely high match")
- **Priority suggestions** — Who to reach out to first and why

### Hiring decision support

Mira provides structured comparisons and analysis to support your hiring decisions, including competitive landscape context and candidate availability insights.

## Training Mira

Beyond the built-in skills, you can customize Mira's behavior:

### Explicit training

Define specific preferences and criteria that Mira should follow:

```
Always prioritize candidates with startup experience over big-company backgrounds
for engineering roles.
```

### Learning from workflows

Mira observes how you interact — which candidates you prioritize, what follow-up questions you ask, how you refine searches — and adapts its behavior over time.

<Info>
Training is scoped to your account. Your preferences and patterns are never shared with other users.
</Info>
