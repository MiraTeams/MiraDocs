## Sign up

Mira is currently in private beta. Visit [mira.day](https://mira.day) to join the waitlist or sign in if you already have access.

## Your first candidate search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new task">
    Click **New Task** in the sidebar. You'll see a text input where you can describe what you're looking for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the role">
    Write your requirements in natural language. Be as specific as you like:

    ```
    Looking for a senior full-stack engineer with React and Node.js experience,
    based in Berlin or open to remote work, ideally from a fast-growing startup.
    ```

    <Tip>The more context you provide — skills, location, seniority, industry — the better Mira's results will be.</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results">
    Mira will search across multiple data sources and return a structured candidate list. Each result includes:
    - Name and current role
    - Background and qualifications
    - Match analysis against your requirements

    Results are exportable as structured data files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine your search">
    Continue the conversation to narrow results:

    ```
    Show me only candidates with published research papers.
    ```

    Mira remembers context within the task and refines accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
