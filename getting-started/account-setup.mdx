## Sign in or sign up

Visit [mira.day/sign-in](https://mira.day/sign-in) to get started. Mira supports three sign-in methods:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Google" icon="google">
    One-click sign in with your Google account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Microsoft" icon="microsoft">
    One-click sign in with your Microsoft account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    Enter your email address and follow the verification flow.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
Sign in and sign up use the same page. If you don't have an account yet, one will be created automatically on your first sign-in.
</Info>

## Your workspace

After signing in, you'll land on the main task screen at [mira.day/task](https://mira.day/task). The interface consists of:

- **Sidebar** (left) — Task list, New Task button, and your account menu
- **Main area** (center) — Task conversation or the home screen with suggested scenarios

## Account settings

Click your name at the bottom-left corner of the sidebar to access:

| Setting | Options |
| --- | --- |
| **Language** | English, Simplified Chinese (简体中文) |
| **Theme** | Light, Dark, System |
| **Sign Out** | End your current session |

## Signing out

Select **Sign Out** from the account menu. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page and can log back in at any time — your tasks and data are preserved.
